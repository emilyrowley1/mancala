Mancala
- 8 pockets on each side
- 1 end pocket - score
- each pocket starts with 4 marbles

game play
- players take turns
- a player can empty any pocket on their own side by removing all marbles and placing one in each of the    pockets following
- a player never places a marble in the opposite players pocket


rules
1. if the last marble ends in an empty pocket the marbles in the pocket across from it is emptied into that players end pocket
2. if the last marble of the players turn ends in their end pocket they get another turn
3. if a player empties all the marbles on their side the game is finished and they get the remaining marbles


classes
- pocket
- end pocket : pocket
- player pocket : pocket
- director
